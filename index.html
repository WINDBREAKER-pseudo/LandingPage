About Us

We Design, Build Futures & Shape Digital Professionals.

MSU-MCEST began as the Maigo High School in 1948, established through the collective efforts of the Maigo community. Over decades, it evolved into the Maigo Trade School under Republic Act No. 2408, and later became part of the Mindanao State University system.

Today, as MSU-Maigo College of Education, Science, and Technology, the institution stands as a premier public college committed to excellence in instruction, research, and community service — expanding into science, technology, and innovation for regional development.
